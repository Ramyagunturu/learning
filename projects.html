My Project is about counterfiet of voting using
"Blockchain Technology" I used biometric aunthetication to verify
each voter through fingerprint or face ids which ensure
general voting and voters can vote only once After verification
the vote is stored on the Blockchain which is secured and tamper proof ledger.

Blockchain makes it impossible to modify or add fake
voters because every vote becomes a permanent block linked to others.
By combining Biometric + Blockchain we prevent fake voting,
duplicate voting and result manipulation, making the entire
election process more secure and transparent.
